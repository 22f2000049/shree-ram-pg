A Personal Message

Home Away from Home — Our Founding Philosophy

"I started Shree Ram with one simple belief — that every person who leaves their family to build a better future deserves to be treated with dignity, warmth, and care."

Back in 2015, when I first opened our doors to 12 residents in a modest building in Civil Lines, my only goal was to offer what I had yearned for as a student from a small town — a safe, clean, and genuinely caring place to call home.

Today, Shree Ram serves over 350 residents across two wings and manages hospitality operations for 25+ corporate and institutional clients. But our core promise has never changed: every resident is a guest, not a number.

We are not a hotel chain. We are not a real estate company. We are a family — and we take the responsibility of being someone's home very seriously.

Care Over Commerce We prioritize the well-being of our residents above all business metrics.
Safety is Non-Negotiable Especially for our girls' wing — a parent's peace of mind is our highest responsibility.
Excellence Every Day We hold ourselves to the same standards we would want if our own children lived here.
Community First A thriving community of residents makes Shree Ram more than just a building — it makes it a home.
— Shri Neeraj Dubey, Founder
Our Story

Nine Years of Growth & Trust

2015 — The Beginning
First PG Opens in Civil Lines

Shree Ram PG opens with 12 residents in a rented building. The founder personally manages all operations — cooking, cleaning, security — with one clear mission: make everyone feel at home.

2016 — Girls Wing Launches
Safe Spaces for Women

Recognising the need for truly secure accommodation for female students, a dedicated Girls Wing is launched with a female warden, biometric access, and an all-women support team.

2018 — Hospitality Services Division
Expanding Beyond PG

Shree Ram Hospitality Services is formally launched, offering catering and facility management to two local colleges. This marks the beginning of B2B operations.

2020 — Navigating the Pandemic
Zero Evictions. Full Support.

During COVID-19, Shree Ram ensured no resident was evicted. Free meals were provided to those unable to pay, earning the brand lifelong loyalty and community trust.

2022 — Corporate Partnerships
25+ Corporate Clients

The hospitality division crosses 25 corporate and institutional clients, including an IT company guest house and a 300-bed nursing college hostel management contract.

2024 — Today & Beyond
350+ Residents · Growing Strong

Shree Ram PG now serves 350+ residents across two wings and manages 10,000+ meals per month for clients. The vision: become Airoli, Navi Mumbai's most recognised name in trusted living.
