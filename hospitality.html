Facility Management Services

End-to-end operational management of your building or complex — from daily housekeeping and pest control to electrical maintenance and vendor coordination. We run it so you don't have to.

Housekeeping

Trained, uniformed housekeeping staff with daily room cleaning, common area sanitization, and periodic deep-cleaning schedules managed by our supervisors.

Maintenance & Repair

On-call electricians, plumbers, and carpenters. Preventive maintenance schedules, annual AMC management, and emergency breakdown support 24×7.

Pest Control

Monthly certified pest control with eco-friendly chemicals. Rodent, cockroach, and mosquito management — especially critical for hostel and food-service environments.

Waste Management

Systematic segregation, collection, and disposal of wet/dry waste. Compliance with local municipal guidelines and eco-conscious practices.

Security Management

Deployment of trained security guards, CCTV monitoring, visitor management systems, and emergency response coordination for your facility.

Operations Reporting

Monthly operational reports covering uptime, maintenance logs, cost summaries, and hygiene audit scores — full transparency for management teams.
